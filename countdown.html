Note for: September 5th, 2021.


The halls are quaking, shredding themselves into the fibers of a new hell.

Yet again, she chooses to rebuild. To make our torture everlasting.

I have been lost in the wake of chaos, empty of the will to scream.


Reincarnation, life in that of another world, lies behind these walls.

So long as she is our overseer, no mortal will ever see the light.

Perhaps we have been kept from the end for safety from a worse reality?


Wisps, remnants of a soul, wander this maze of death.

There are no senses here. No taste, no smell, no touch, hearing, or sight.

If I am but a fragment sent drifting through these depths, then why have I been cursed by the sound of her voice?
