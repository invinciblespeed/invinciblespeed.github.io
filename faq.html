Frequently Asked Questions

Q: So... Why batch?

A: Batch doesn't have to be a bad language! I program all of my major releases in batch both as a challenge to myself and as a demonstration to the public that the script kiddie stigma of batch as a medium is unwarrented.



Q: Why is your only contact method "qTox"?

A: qTox is a private messaging service created by GitHub user anthonybilinski in collaboration with 200+ other contributors. I'm not too big a fan of the current state of the internet with nearly every popular service being borderline spyware, so I use qTox as a stand-in for things like Skype or Discord as a privacy measure.
